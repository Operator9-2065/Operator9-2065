Welcome to the HNSS Computer Science Club!




This webpage is where the HNSS Computer Science Club (or CSC) will post updates, accomplishments, news, events and progress.

The CSC values learning through experience and work and is open to members of all skill levels.
The CSC offers tutoring through our experiences executive team and opprotunities for resume-boosting opprotunities,
like competitions, developement of apps/websites/games and more.

Additionally, the CSC has monthly topics to learn and explore computer science concepts like
cybersecurity, web/app developement, algorithm building/problem solving, hardware and more. Each month, the club is divided into groups and can compete with each other in a culminating activity that encompasses all concepts taught in that month.

Meetings occur each Friday in room 338 at 1525 and our classroom code is z6ikrcr.


This month's topic:

October/November: Web developement

During October, members of the CSC will be working on learning HTML, CSS and Javascript.

After extensive learning and various problem-solving activities completed, members will be split into groups in the final week to compete in the culminating challenge.

Each group will create a website that will be reviews and judged by executives and peers (no group member can vote for their own group).


For more detals, please see the monthly topics page. All previous topic material and competition results will be posted there as well.

Our Goal:

The CSC's goal is to educate those who are interested in computer science, in all ages, levels of interest, levels of skill and experience,
as well as prepare members to strengthen their career/university applications and compete with distinction.
