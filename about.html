What is the HNSS Computer Science Club?



What do programming, making new friends and solving challenges have in common?

The HNSS computer science club! Every Friday after school, the HNSS Computer Science Club (or CSC) will be meeting to work on fun activities, learn computer science concepts and prepare for programming competitions. The CSC encourages learning by doing, preparing activities, events and challenges within each month relating to computer science concepts.

Whether you are interested in learning more about computer science, seeking homework help or just want to hang out with like-minded people, we encourage you to join. If time allows, some executive positions may be open for various opprotunities to be more responsible within the club.

Meetings occur every Friday at 1530

NO PROGRAMMING EXPERIENCE IS REQUIRED!



The HNSS Computer Science Club offers many mediums for learning experiences and opprotunities like:


Monthly topics


Every month, the CSC explores diffrent concepts within computer science.
Some topics include cybersecurity, web developement, careers, problem-solving, data analysis, hardware and many more.

Typically, members divide themselves into groups and compete for prizes in the monthly competition!

For more details, please see the monthly challenges page.




Interviews

The CSC occasionally will interview those with experience in computer science.
The interviews can include post-secondary education experiences, career advice, advice in studies and personal stories.

Some sessions are recorded and accessed at any time.

For more details, please see the community page for recorded sessions and the news page for updates on upcoming events.






Competitions


The CSC allows its members to compete in both club-organized and official competitions. Every month, members are split into groups and compete for the ultimate display of what they learned that month.

Additionally, members of the CSC are encourages to compete in the Canadian Computing Competition that is hosted in HNSS, as well as in other official competitions.

Members are given the resouces and aid to perform with proficiency in these competition, like tutoring, challening problems, remarks from those with experience, mock tests and more.

For more details on upcoming club or third-party competitions, please see the news page.





Tutoring


The CSC creates an environement when everyone at any skill level can improve, either by themselves or with the help of another.

The executives have experience with programming in many languages and are more than willing to aid for various coding problems.

The most popular languages amongst our experienced 2022-2023 executives are C++, Java, HTML/CSS and Python
