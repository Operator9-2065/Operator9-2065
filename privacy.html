Privacy policy



The HNSS computer science club does not collect any personal data.



The CSC may however may use cookies in the following way:



We may use Google Analytics. This enables us to monitor and analyze web traffic so that we can improve the website and user experience. Google Analytics collects anonymous user data and stores it for a limited time per their privacy policy. If you are uncomfortable with Google Analytics, you might consider a Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.





If you are not fine with our use of cookies you can either disable them or avoid using our website.
